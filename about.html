Our story

Capital Crown Lab exists because DMV dentists need a lab that operates in their time zone. The economics of offshore lab work look attractive on a per-unit basis, but they hide the cost of the round-trip: the multi-week remake cycle, the language barrier on case questions, the inability to inspect a case in person before it is shipped to the patient. We exist to give DMV practices a serious local alternative.

We built the lab around three commitments. First, we are a digital lab — CAD/CAM is the default, and every case is documented digitally from intake through delivery. Second, we are accountable — a technician's name goes on every case, and the technician who designed the case is the one who handles a remake if it is needed. Third, we are transparent — pricing is published, materials are named on every case envelope, and the remake policy is written down and enforced.
