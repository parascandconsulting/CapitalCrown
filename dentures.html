Dentures

Full and partial denture cases, conventional and digital workflows.

Five-appointment full denture protocol. Cast-metal and flexible partials. Same-week relines and repairs.

Full Dentures

Our standard full denture workflow runs five appointments and three to four weeks. We support both the conventional impression workflow and the digital workflow on supported scanner platforms.

The appointments

  1. Final impression. You take a border-molded final impression; we pour the master cast.
  2. Bite registration. Custom tray + occlusion rim sent back for the VDO, midline, smile line, and shade.
  3. Wax try-in. Tooth setup mounted for the patient to evaluate phonetics, aesthetics, and bite.
  4. Process & finish. We process the final, polish, and deliver.
  5. Insertion & adjust. You insert; we are available the same week for any adjustment-related questions.

Tooth options

Three tiers, set at account opening or per case:

  • Standard2-layer acrylic, A–D shade range
  • PremiumMulti-layer composite, broader shade range, better wear
  • Hi-aestheticCharacterized anteriors, hand-tinted gingival

Partial Dentures

Cast-metal frames (cobalt-chromium) for the strongest, most rigid partial. Flexible nylon partials when esthetics and patient comfort drive the case. Combination cases on consultation.

Cast-metal partial

  • FrameworkCast Co-Cr alloy
  • RetentionCast clasps, optional precision attachments
  • Appointments4 (impression, framework try-in, wax try-in, insertion)

Flexible partial

  • MaterialNylon thermoplastic (Valplast-style)
  • RetentionEngaged through undercuts; no metal display
  • Appointments3

Reline & Repair

Hard chairside relines and lab-processed hard or soft relines. Most denture repairs — broken acrylic, displaced teeth, broken clasps — turn around in one to two business days.

Immediate denture protocol

For immediate dentures we model the extraction sockets digitally where the scan is available, or trim the cast on a conventional workflow. Surgical guide for any teeth being extracted at the same appointment can be ordered alongside.
