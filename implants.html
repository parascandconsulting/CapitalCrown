Restoration Types

Custom titanium abutment

Designed in 3Shape Implant Studio against the soft-tissue contour and emergence profile from your scan. Milled in-house from grade-5 titanium.

Hybrid Ti-base + zirconia abutment

For anterior cases that need a metal-free supragingival appearance. Ti-base bonded to a milled zirconia abutment with the appropriate cement.

Screw-retained implant crown

Default for retrievability. Crown bonded directly to a Ti-base. Available in monolithic zirconia or milled e.max depending on position.

Cement-retained implant crown

Custom abutment + zirconia or e.max crown. Used when the access channel cannot be aesthetically managed for screw retention.

Implant bridges & All-on-X frameworks

Multi-unit implant bridges from 3 to 14 units, on Ti-base substructures. Full-arch hybrids and conversion prostheses on consultation. Verification jigs available for cases requiring passive fit confirmation before the final restoration.

What to send

  • Intraoral scan with scan bodies in place
  • Opposing scan
  • Bite registration (intraoral or sectioned bite)
  • Shade and stump shade if metal substructure is involved
  • Prescription with implant system, platform diameter, and tissue height
  • Photograph if anterior or aesthetic case

Design preview on every implant case

By default we send a design preview screenshot to the prescribing dentist before milling on every implant restoration. If you would prefer to skip the preview to save a day on turnaround, indicate on the prescription.

Implant case waiting on a lab?

Send the scan body export and the prescription and we will confirm system identification and quote within one business day.
