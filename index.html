Technology stack

A fully digital lab. Conventional case work still accepted.

We accept scanner exports from every major intraoral system, plus PVS, alginate, and triple-tray impressions when a case calls for it.

01 · DESIGN

3Shape Dental System + exocad

Dual-CAD environment lets us match the design tool to the case type. Anatomy libraries are kept current, occlusion is checked against the antagonist scan, and contacts are validated digitally before milling.

02 · MANUFACTURE

5-axis milling + IPS pressing

In-house 5-axis dry mills for zirconia and PMMA, wet milling for glass-ceramics, and conventional pressing for e.max. Surgical guides and models on a calibrated SLA printer.

03 · FINISH

Hand-glazed, hand-polished

A certified dental technician finishes every restoration. Anterior cases get individual characterization. Posteriors get a documented occlusion check on an articulated model before they leave.

How a case moves through the lab

Submit. Design. Mill. Finish. Deliver.

You see every step. Case status is available to your front desk on request.

01

Submit

Scan upload through 3Shape Communicate, DDX, or our portal. Or schedule a courier pickup for impressions and bite records.

02

Intake

Case is logged, prescription confirmed, and any clarifying questions are sent back the same business day. We do not start work on incomplete cases.

03

Design

CAD design in 3Shape or exocad. Design previews available to you on request before milling.

04

Manufacture

Mill, press, sinter, or print, depending on material. Quality control occurs at each stage, not just at the end.

05

Finish & QC

Margin fit, contacts, occlusion, shade, and surface finish are checked and signed off by a CDT.

06

Deliver

Courier delivery to your practice with a tracking link. Case envelope includes the prescription, the design summary, and a remake policy reference.
