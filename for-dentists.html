Why partner with Capital Crown Lab

You are reading this because something about your current lab arrangement is not working. Maybe it is turnaround. Maybe it is communication. Maybe it is the offshore round-trip that turned a one-week remake into three weeks. Here is what changes when your cases come to us.

Local turnaround

Five to ten business days on standard crown and bridge work. Same-week remake on any case that does not seat. Couriered delivery to your practice, not mail-in shipping.

A named technician on every case

Cases are not anonymized through a pool. The technician who designed your case is reachable by phone or by email, and is the person who handles the remake if one is needed.

Material transparency

Every case ships with a one-page summary that names the material, the manufacturing process, and the technician who finished it. If your patient has a question about what is in their mouth, you can answer it in a sentence.

Honest pricing

We publish wholesale-to-practice ranges on the services page. The price you see is the price you pay; rush is opt-in; there is no "setup fee" or "shade fee" hidden in the invoice.

How partnering works

01

Account opening

Email us or use the contact form. We send a one-page account form for your billing contact, shipping addresses, and scanner preferences.

02

Scanner integration

We are added to your scanner's destination list. We send instructions specific to iTero, TRIOS, Medit, Carestream, or Primescan; setup takes 10 minutes.

03

First case

Send a test case — a standard single-unit crown is ideal. We document turnaround, communication, and fit so you can evaluate the partnership on a real case.

04

Ongoing

Net-30 terms unlock after the third completed case. Volume tiers and dedicated technician assignment for practices over 15 cases/month.
