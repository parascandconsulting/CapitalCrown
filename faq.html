FAQ

Practical answers for dentists evaluating a new lab.

Turnaround, materials, courier, HIPAA, scanner compatibility, and account terms.

Turnaround

What is your standard turnaround on crowns and bridges?
Five business days on full-contour zirconia and milled e.max single units. Six business days on pressed e.max. Seven business days on PFM and layered zirconia. Bridges add two to three business days per additional unit. Turnaround starts from receipt of complete case, not from submission — incomplete prescriptions are flagged at intake.
Do you offer rush service?
Yes. Next-business-day rush on supported case types adds 50% to the base fee; two-business-day rush adds 25%. Rush availability depends on day-of capacity; we will confirm at intake.
What is the turnaround on dentures?
Full dentures run three to four weeks across five appointments. Cast-metal partials run three weeks across four appointments. Flexible partials run two to three weeks. Immediate dentures run two weeks. Relines and repairs typically turn around in one to five business days.

Materials

What zirconia do you use?
Multilayer zirconia blanks with graduated translucency. Posterior cases default to high-strength formulations (~1,200 MPa); anterior cases default to higher-translucency formulations (~850 MPa). We are happy to substitute on request.
Pressed e.max or milled e.max?
Both. Pressed gives the best surface and the most refined finish but adds a day to turnaround. Milled is faster and is the default for posterior single units. We will choose based on the case unless you specify.
Are your materials FDA-compliant?
All ceramics, alloys, acrylics, and resins we use are FDA-cleared for their indicated use. FDA registration of the laboratory itself is in progress as part of our launch compliance work.

Coverage

What is your courier coverage area?
Northern Virginia (Arlington, Alexandria, Fairfax, Tysons, McLean, Vienna, Falls Church, Reston), suburban Maryland (Bethesda, Rockville, Silver Spring, Chevy Chase), and the District (NW DC, Capitol Hill, downtown). Outer-DMV practices are served via overnight courier; we will confirm at account opening.
Do you accept practices outside the DMV?
Yes, on a digital-first basis. If you can submit cases through a supported scanner, distance does not change the turnaround. We add a flat overnight shipping fee for delivery outside the courier zone.

Billing

What are your payment terms?
Introductory accounts pay first case on delivery, Net-15 thereafter. Standing accounts move to Net-30 after the third completed case. Volume discounts apply at 15+ cases per month.
How do you handle remakes?
Remakes for margin, contact, or occlusion errors verified at intake are no-charge. Patient-driven changes (color, shape, anatomy) after final delivery are quoted as adjustments at a reduced fee.

HIPAA & data

How do you handle PHI in scan files and prescriptions?
Scans and prescriptions are received through HIPAA-compliant channels (scanner manufacturer portals like 3Shape Communicate, DDX, MyAligntech, or our case portal) and stored encrypted at rest. Access is limited to the case team. We will sign a BAA at account opening.
Do you keep our case files?
We retain digital case files for seven years to support remakes, replacements, and continuity of care. Files can be deleted on practice request, subject to remake warranty implications.

Scanner compatibility

My scanner is not on your integrations list. Can I still send digitally?
Yes. If you can export STL/PLY/OBJ from your system you can upload through our case portal alongside the bite scan and a prescription. We will add native integration for your scanner on request — new integrations typically go live within a business week.
