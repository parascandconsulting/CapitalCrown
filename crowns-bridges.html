Crowns & bridges

The materials, the prep guidance, the turnaround.

Full-contour zirconia, layered zirconia, IPS e.max, PFM, and PFG. Single units through six-unit bridges. Anterior and posterior.

Materials

The crown and bridge case mix at most general practices is roughly 60% full-contour zirconia, 25% IPS e.max, and the remainder split across PFM, layered zirconia, and the occasional Maryland or resin-bonded bridge. We carry all of it.

Full-contour zirconia (monolithic)

The workhorse material for posterior single units. Multilayer zirconia blanks give a graduated translucency from cervical to incisal, which avoids the chalky look of earlier generations. Flexural strength runs 850–1,200 MPa depending on the blank, so it tolerates parafunction. We mill, sinter, and glaze in-house.

  • Strength850–1,200 MPa
  • Minimum prep clearance1.0 mm occlusal · 0.5 mm axial
  • CementationConventional or resin
  • IndicationPosterior single units, short-span posterior bridges

IPS e.max (lithium disilicate)

Lithium disilicate remains the strongest aesthetic option for anteriors and premolars. Pressed e.max gives the best chairside polish; milled e.max gives the fastest turnaround. We do both and will recommend based on the case.

  • Strength~400 MPa
  • Minimum prep clearance1.5 mm occlusal · 1.0 mm axial
  • CementationAdhesive (etch + bond)
  • IndicationAnterior single units, premolars, veneers, inlays/onlays

Layered zirconia

Zirconia core with feldspathic porcelain hand-layered on the buccal. We use this when the case calls for anterior aesthetics that monolithic cannot deliver and the strength of an e.max alternative is not sufficient.

PFM (porcelain-fused-to-metal)

Available with high-noble, noble, or non-noble substructures. We default to noble. Specify metal alloy at submission if your practice has a preference; we will quote.

Bridges

Single-unit cantilever through six-unit anterior or posterior spans in zirconia. Longer spans on consultation. Maryland bridges and resin-bonded retainers available for conservative replacement of single anterior teeth.

Prep guides & reference

Material-specific prep guides for zirconia, e.max, and PFM are available on request. We can also send a wax-up or digital design preview before milling for any case — just request it on the prescription.
